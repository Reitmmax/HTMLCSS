Simple Omelettes

Omelettes are one of my favorite foods. Whenever I go out to breakfast place, I almost always order one. Sometimes I make some myself. This is my recipe.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 2 mushrooms
  • a bell pepper
  • a handful of shredded cheedar

Recipe

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and wisk them until the yolks are not visible
  2. Heat up a pan and put the butter in the pan. Spread it across the pan.
  3. Slice the mushrooms and bell peppers to a small size
  4. Pour the eggs into then pan when the butter is spread and melted
  5. Add the shredded cheese to the egg after it starts to solidify
  6. When the cheese is melted add the mushrooms and peppers to the mix
  7. Carefully fold the egg in half, it is very fragile.
